Edward Jenner

Here are some facts about Edward Jenner

Known as the "father of immunology", Edward Jenner created and tested the first smallpox vaccine in 1796.1

In 1979 the World Health Organization declared smallpox an eradicated disease.2

1. An Inquiry into the Causes and Effects of the Variolae Vaccinae, Edward Jenner.
2. World Health Organization (2001). "Smallpox".
